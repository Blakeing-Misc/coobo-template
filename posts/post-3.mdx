##### The Importance of Regular Dental Checkups

Going to the dentist may not be anyone's favorite activity, but it's important for maintaining good oral health. Regular dental checkups can help to prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues. Plus, they can catch any potential problems early on, when they're easier and less expensive to treat.
